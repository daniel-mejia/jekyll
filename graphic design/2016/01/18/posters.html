Zambrana Health Coach

Andrea Zambrana is a health coach who specializes in helping women achieve healthier lives. This project consisted on designing three e-books and their respective online selling pages complete with web graphics. Each e-books had edited images and illustrated exercise routines. The sale pages were designed in WordPress.

For more information on these e-books you can visit Zambrana Health Coach's website or each of Gym, Home or HIIT exercise e-books webpages.

  • Client: Zambrana Health Coach
  • Date: June 2015
  • Service: Graphic Design, Web Design

Island Publishing Services

While living in the British Virgin Islands I worked for Island Publishing Services, a tourist guide publishing company. Responsible for the design of publications, maintaining the website and leading social media efforts. I gave the magazine a fresh look to attract new readers from tourists visiting the island and proud locals alike.

My biggest challenge while working with Island Publishing Services was having to document with pictures some of the island’s most famous spots and fun activities then share them with future visitors (what a drag :P).

  • Client: Island Publishing Services
  • Date: November 2015
  • Service: Graphic Design, Web Design
